Australia has a rich sporting culture with many popular sports. Australian Rules Football, also known as AFL, is the most popular sport in Australia. Cricket is another major sport, with Australia having one of the world's best cricket teams. Rugby League and Rugby Union are both popular in Australia. Soccer, known as football in Australia, is growing in popularity. Tennis is popular, with the Australian Open being one of the four Grand Slam tournaments. Swimming is very popular due to Australia's coastal lifestyle. Surfing is an iconic Australian sport, with world-class surf breaks along the coast. Netball is a popular women's sport in Australia. Basketball has grown in popularity, with the National Basketball League. Australian athletes have excelled in the Olympics, particularly in swimming, cycling, and athletics. The Melbourne Cup is Australia's most famous horse race and is known as the race that stops a nation.
