Sydney is the largest and most populous city in Australia. It is located on the east coast of Australia and is the capital of New South Wales. Sydney is famous for its iconic Sydney Opera House and Sydney Harbour Bridge. The city has a vibrant cultural scene with numerous museums, galleries, and theaters. Melbourne is Australia's second-largest city and the capital of Victoria. It is known for its coffee culture, street art, and sporting events. Melbourne has been ranked as one of the world's most liveable cities. Brisbane is the capital of Queensland and is known for its warm climate and outdoor lifestyle. Perth is the capital of Western Australia and is one of the most isolated major cities in the world. Adelaide is the capital of South Australia and is known for its wine regions and festivals. Canberra is the capital city of Australia and is located in the Australian Capital Territory.
