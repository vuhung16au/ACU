Atlassian takes on Google, Microsoft with $1b AI-powered browser play

Paul Smith and Tess Bennett
Updated Sep 5, 2025 – 1.22pm, first published at 6.51am
 or Subscribe to save article

Atlassian is taking a billion-dollar plunge into the browser market, buying up an American start-up that it hopes will lock users into its workplace software and putting it in competition with Google and Microsoft.

Atlassian said it would acquire New York-headquartered The Browser Company, behind the Dia and Arc browsers, for $US610 million ($936 million) and will use its tech to create a platform that will connect the various workplace applications that the company already provides.
